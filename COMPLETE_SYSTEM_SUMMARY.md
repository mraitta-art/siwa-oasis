## 🚀 **HOW TO USE**

### **Using Blog Templates:**

1. **Go to**: http://localhost:3000/admin/blog-templates
2. **Browse** 10 pre-built blog components
3. **Filter** by category or search
4. **Click** "Copy Code" to get HTML/CSS
5. **Use** in your pages or form builder

### **Managing Carousel:**

1. **Go to**: http://localhost:3000/admin/hero-carousel
2. **Click** "+ Add Slide"
3. **Fill in**:
   - Image URL
   - Title & Subtitle
   - CTA Text & Link
   - Animation style
4. **Click** "Save Slide"
5. **Manage** slides (edit/delete/reorder)

### **Creating Blog Posts:**

1. **Go to**: http://localhost:3000/admin/blog
2. **Click** "+ New Post"
3. **Fill in** the editor form
4. **Publish** or save as draft
